Welcome to Rome!

The Backstab

The ride starts off going up a steep slope. Once reaching the top, you ride down the slope and start going up and down, aboveground and underground (23 times).

The Bath Bomb

The Bath Bomb is a recreation of the famous Roman Bathhouse with one of the pirate ship looping rides.

Macellum Marketplace

My shop will look like a traditional Roman market. These markets looked like food shops at your local mall: they have a counter at the entrance instead of having a shop that you can walk around in. I will make them out of concrete and I will sell wine.

Eataly

I will serve traditional Roman Cuisine in this restaurant along with some basic food for picky eaters. I will also base the design off of regular eating locations in Rome. Like for example, taverns; We will also serve wine as Ancient Rome drank wine to connect to their gods.

Colosseum Fights

The Colosseum was a massive part of Rome. It provided entertainment to many and the idea of the Colosseum is what I aim to recreate. We will have daily fights at noon which will be hosted by many trained professionals.

Colosseum Hotel

The hotel will be underground and each room will look like a traditional Roman room. The hotel part will be underground, while the actual Colosseum will be used for daily fights at noon.

Julius Caesar

Major Historical Leader of Rome.

Julius Caesar will run around the park taking photos and giving high-fives to the park guests. At 1:00 after the Colosseum fight is over, a pack of assassins will run after Julius Caesar trying to “stab” him.

Augustus

Historical Leader of Rome

Augustus will run around the park picking 2 people everyday. These 2 people will get exclusive merchandise or a small cash prize.
